The Method

Practice, not lessons.

English4B2B is not another conversation app, and not a school. Each course is a focused practice tool — built around the English moments your career turns on.

i.

Specific situations

Every drill is drawn from the English that corporate and international work actually requires — meeting status updates, presentation Q&A, interview questions, AI prompts — not textbook dialogues about restaurants and weather.

ii.

AI-native feedback

You speak or write a response. The AI returns commentary specific enough to act on — clarity, register, word choice, structure — within seconds, not days. Repeat until the pattern holds.

iii.

Self-paced. Lifetime access.

Buy a course once; come back whenever the next high-stakes moment is on your calendar. No cohort dates. No waitlists. The tool waits for you, not the other way around.

Questions

Common questions, answered directly.

What level of English do I need?
Intermediate to advanced. These are practice tools for professionals already working in English who want the language to hold up in higher-stakes moments — not a beginner course.
How is this different from ChatGPT or a conversation app?
The drills, prompts, and feedback rubrics are the product. Every exercise is built around a specific corporate moment, and the AI is instructed to evaluate the answer the way a senior colleague would — not as a friendly chat partner. You get pattern-level feedback, not just a reply.
In what language is the material?
In English, with Portuguese notes where useful. The objective is to think and work in English; Portuguese is there to shorten the bridge, not to live on.
How does access work?
Buy a course once, come back whenever you need it. Lifetime access to the drills, simulator, and any future additions to that course. No subscription, no renewal.
Is there a company / team option?
Yes. For teams of six or more, English4B2B runs private workshops with curriculum tailored to the company's sector and stage. Contact directly to discuss.
